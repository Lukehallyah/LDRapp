How This Works

Have you ever wondered what types of things that you could do with you partner over long distance? Maybe you're both home after work and want to try something new? This activity generator is just the thing you need to spice up your evening! With the click of a button, an activity is generated and described in an easy no pressure way to bring lots of excitement and connection for you both! The goal of is to be able to foster closer connection and fun between couples who want more than watching tv or scrolling thru social media. It's about fostering that connection and bringing closeness in a world consumed by media. So have fun with your partner and see what kinds of cool things you can do together!
